Avg. Temp dataset comparison
The graph visualizations presented here offer a comprehensive insight into temperature patterns, showcasing data from two distinct sources: Open Meteo and Rhodes University's on-site weather data collection. The Open Meteo data illustrates temperature trends in a broader geographical context, providing a valuable overview of temperature variations for a Grahamstown (Makhanda). In contrast, Rhodes University's on-site weather data provides more localized and precise temperature information, allowing us to observe microclimate fluctuations. The disparity in observed temperatures between these datasets can be attributed to several factors, including variations in data collection methodologies, the influence of local topography, and potential microclimatic effects. While Open Meteo data offers a broader perspective, Rhodes University's on-site data provides a detailed understanding of the unique temperature dynamics at a specific location. Together, these visualizations enable a comprehensive analysis of temperature trends, catering to both regional and localized meteorological insights.
